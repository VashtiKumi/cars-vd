About AutoElite

Redefining the luxury automotive experience since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005, AutoElite began with a simple vision: to provide discerning clients with an unparalleled automotive experience. What started as a boutique dealership with a handful of luxury vehicles has grown into one of the most respected names in premium automotive sales and service.

Our founder, Jonathan Reynolds, a lifelong car enthusiast with over 30 years of experience in the automotive industry, established AutoElite with the belief that purchasing and owning a luxury vehicle should be as exceptional as the cars themselves.

Today, AutoElite continues to uphold this founding principle, offering an exclusive selection of the world's finest automobiles and a level of service that exceeds expectations at every turn.
